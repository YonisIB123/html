• Name: Nelson Mandela
  • Born: July 18, 1918
  • Died: December 5, 2013
  • Nationality: South African
  • Profession: Anti-Apartheid Leader, Politician
  1. First Black President of South Africa
  2. Leader of the Anti-Apartheid Movement
  3. Spent 27 years in prison fighting injustice
  4. Won the Nobel Peace Prize in 1993
  5. Global symbol of peace and equality
